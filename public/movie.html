Ashley's Movie Rankings

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Along Came a Spider

"Along Came a Spider" is a psychological thriller released in 2001, directed by Lee Tamahori and starring Morgan Freeman as the protagonist, Alex Cross. The movie is based on the novel of the same name by James Patterson, part of the Alex Cross series..

Hocus Pocus

"Hocus Pocus" is a 1993 American comedy-horror fantasy film directed by Kenny Ortega, centering around three witch sisters—Winifred, Sarah, and Mary Sanderson—who are inadvertently resurrected by a teenage boy in Salem, Massachusetts, on Halloween night. The witches, played by Bette Midler, Sarah Jessica Parker, and Kathy Najimy, plan to use a spell to suck the life force from children to maintain their youth and immortality.

John Wick

"John Wick" is a 2014 action thriller directed by Chad Stahelski, starring Keanu Reeves as the titular character, a retired hitman seeking vengeance for the killing of his dog, a last gift from his deceased wife. The film is renowned for its meticulously choreographed fight sequences, stylized visuals, and its portrayal of a unique, intricate underworld with its own set of rules and codes of conduct.
